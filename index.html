Introduction

The Journal of Materials Intelligence and Computing is an open-access platform dedicated to publishing high-quality research that bridges multiple scientific disciplines. The journal aims to foster collaboration among researchers in physics, chemistry, materials science, computational science, and related fields.

Aims and Scope

The journal welcomes original research articles, reviews, and short communications in areas including:

  • Nanoscience and Nanotechnology
  • Computational Physics and Chemistry
  • Materials Science and Engineering
  • Theoretical and Applied Physics
  • Interdisciplinary Scientific Innovations

Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit manuscripts via email. All submissions must be original and not under consideration elsewhere. Manuscripts will undergo peer review before publication.
