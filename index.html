About the Journal

The Journal of Materials Intelligence and Computing (JMIC) is an interdisciplinary, open-access, peer-reviewed journal dedicated to advancing research at the convergence of materials science, Physics, Chemistry, AI, machine learning, computational modeling, and related disciplines.
